Why Social Distancing is Important for Indians?



Well, it has been 60 days since India recorded the first case of coronavirus on 30 January, but the growth of cases started in March. In Feb, India has seen only 3 cases and now it is 1053 (940 active, 86 recovered, 27 deceased).



Now exponential growth has started as you can see the no. of new cases in the last two weeks is 3 times more.The no. of cases is less in India but growth is high. let's see a comparison between India and the top infected countries around the world in Week 4.

India can be very susceptible to coronavirus because population density of India is very high and health care ranking is very low.let's understand this by the below graph

If you are thinking I missed the ranking of India in Healthcare ranking Graph.well this is not the case, India is not ranked in Top 150 Best Healthcare in the World by Population 2020.

As you can see, the above statics are not a good sign for India. So, How can India win this battle with coronavirus?The answer is "Social Distancing". ( Social distancing, or physical distancing, is a set of non-pharmaceutical interventions or measures taken to prevent the spread of a contagious disease by maintaining a physical distance between people and reducing the number of times people come into close contact with each other) **Source: Wikipedia
