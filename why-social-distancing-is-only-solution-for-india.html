Why Social distancing is the only solution for India to fight with Covid19?

Well, it has been 60 days since India recorded the first case of coronavirus on 30 January, but the growth of cases started in March. In Feb, India has seen only 3 cases and now it is 1053 (940 active, 86 recovered, 27 deceased).

**Data Source Covid 19 India-Tracker

Now exponential growth has started as you can see the no. of new cases in the last two weeks is 3 times more.The no. of cases is less in India but growth is high.
let's see a comparison between India and the top infected countries around the world in Week 4.

**Data Source Wikipedia

India is susceptible to coronavirus because the population density of India is high and health care ranking is low.let's understand this by the below graph.

**Data Source World Population Review

If you are thinking I missed the ranking of India in Healthcare ranking Graph.well this is not the case, India is not ranked in Top 150 Best Healthcare in the World by Population 2020.
Some research is saying that the no. of cases can rise to 10 lakhs till the end of May in India but what about health care services?

**Source The Print

So, How can India win this battle with coronavirus?
The answer is "Social Distancing".
( Social distancing, or physical distancing, is a set of non-pharmaceutical interventions or measures taken to prevent the spread of a contagious disease by maintaining a physical distance between people and reducing the number of times people come into close contact with each other)
**Source Wikipedia

So practice social distancing and Save India.


As you can see, the above stats are not a good sign for India. but we need not panic, there is a lot of positive points for India beyond these data.
*India is still in phase 2 after 4 weeks but all the above countries were in phase 3 at the end of week 4.
*India implement lockdown taking the seriousness of the issue very early than the above countries.
*Around 80% of cases are found in the countries where the average temperature is <15 degree Celsius, but in India, it is around 26-degree celsius and it will increase in the coming days.
